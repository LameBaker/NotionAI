# Project Context

## Problem
Notion costs are high because many users only read content. Goal is to keep writers in Notion and deliver read access through Slack Q&A.

## MVP Strategy
- One Slack bot.
- Notion as source content.
- Google Workspace OU as access source.
- Root-level policy + page-level overrides.

## Pilot Scope
- `HR` root: available to all employees.
- `Development` root: available to Development OU.
- Page-level overrides with Notion ACL tags.
